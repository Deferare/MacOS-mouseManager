# Privacy Policy for MouseManager

Last updated: February 16, 2026

MouseManager ("the App") is developed by deferare. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App processes when you use it.

## Data We Collect

MouseManager does not collect, transmit, store, or sell personal data on developer-operated servers.

The App does not include:

- third-party analytics SDKs
- advertising SDKs
- cross-app tracking technologies

## Data Stored on Your Device

MouseManager stores app preferences locally on your Mac (for example, pointer/scroll/button settings). This local data is used only to apply your chosen behavior and is not sent to deferare.

## Permissions

### Accessibility Permission

MouseManager requires macOS Accessibility permission to intercept and modify mouse/scroll input events.

- Input processing is performed locally on your device.
- MouseManager does not upload your input event data to developer servers.

You can revoke this permission at any time in:
`System Settings > Privacy & Security > Accessibility`.

## In-App Purchases (Donations)

If you choose to support the App via in-app purchase, payment processing is handled by Apple through StoreKit and the App Store.

- MouseManager does not receive your full payment card details.
- Apple may process purchase-related data under Apple's own privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

MouseManager does not share personal data with third parties for advertising or analytics.

Information may be disclosed only if required by law.

## Data Retention

Because MouseManager does not operate user accounts or backend data storage, deferare does not retain personal data from app usage.

Local settings remain on your device until you modify them or remove the App.

## Children's Privacy

MouseManager is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
